Stage 2

Active Champions (Ages 9-12)

Philosophy: Skill Mastery and Team Spirit. This is the "Golden Age" of learning. We introduce structured sports concepts, agility ladders, and healthy competition.

What they learn

  • Fast Reflexes
  • Strategic Thinking
  • Endurance

Parent Note

At this age, children start feeling academic pressure. This hour serves as a natural endorphin boost to keep them focused.

Stage 3

Teen Warriors (Ages 13-16)

Philosophy: Resilience and High Performance. As teens face significant body changes and board exam stress, our program acts as a mental health sanctuary and a physical strength builder.
